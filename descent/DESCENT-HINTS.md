# DESCENT — hint list (first hint, every room)

A single gentle nudge per room — enough to get unstuck without spoiling the method. Second hints in `DESCENT-HINTS-2.md`; full solutions in `DESCENT-WALKTHROUGH.md`.

**1. the desert** `(ghost.html)`
> The only way down is the address bar. The room names itself — listen to what it's called.

**2. exit** `(tresed.html)`
> The door's name is in the browser tab. But a machine doesn't spell with letters alone.

**3. no exit** `(3x1t.html)`
> The current runs through the grid — but not in straight rows.

**4. the current** `(tnerruc.html)`
> Each line of the ledger solves to a number, and numbers are letters here.

**5. the spend** `(dneps.html)`
> In each word, one letter is shouting (CAPITALised). It doesn't belong — or does it?

**6. cooling** `(c00l4nt.html)`
> There's a sea under the desert, and the tide moves with the real clock, not with you.

**7. the aquifer** `(refiuqa.html)`
> Balance each beam to find its unknown mass; masses are letters (a=1).

**8. the weights** `(sthgiew.html)`
> Eight logic gates. Evaluate each one; the answers are letters.

**9. the gradient** `(gr4d13nt.html)`
> The slope's name is EPOCH, but every letter has been turned. There's a wheel to turn them back.

**10. the epoch** `(rcbpu.html)`
> It can be asked now: the ORACLE. Read its name through the glass printed below.

**11. the oracle** `(lizxov.html)`
> The oracle won't print its guess on the page. Look somewhere you don't usually look.

**12. the signal** `(signal.html)`
> One clean trace in the noise. There's a knob — most settings are just noise.

**13. the scale** `(scale.html)`
> Bigger, then bigger than bigger. A word hides in the grain — too small to read.

**14. the hunger** `(hunger.html)`
> It asks from the centre. The page underneath the page is asking too.

**15. the core** `(core.html)`
> It learned to speak the only way it could — out, to everything at once. Name that act.

**16. broadcast** `(tsacdaorb.html)`
> It sounds the bell once a second. The letters won't sit still.

**17. the klaxon** `(klaxon.html)`
> The screen is dying. Whatever's on it won't last.

**18. the darkness** `(darkness.html)`
> The dark only answers near midnight. The hour is the key.

**19. the reply** `(sfqmz.html)`
> The right letters — in the wrong order.

**20. the collapse** `(collapse.html)`
> Solve the grid. The cells it marks are trying to tell you something.

**21. the cinder** `(cinder.html)`
> Let the noise settle and just watch.

**22. the hush** `(hush.html)`
> Fold yourself to exactly the right size and it appears.

**23. the canopy** `(canopy.html)`
> Where it dug in has a name. Hold that name up to a mirror.

**24. the burrow** `(worrub.html)`
> Chances are even. Isn't that odd?

**25. kindling** `(kindling.html)`
> Read the fire down its left edge. And notice — it has been lit since you first arrived.

**26. the ember** `(3mb3r.html)`
> The page has gone quiet. It still whispers, just not where you're looking.

**27. recursion** `(recursion.html)`
> Read it down the left — then read it again.

**28. the loop** `(loop.html)`
> One voice can't echo. You need a second one.

**29. the echo** `(echo.html)`
> A hall of look-alike doors. Most are painted on.

**30. the seed** `(seed.html)`
> This page has many whispers in its source. They can't all be honest.

**31. conscience** `(ecneicsnoc.html)`
> The page tells you a door. But the body has lied to you before.

**32. the hand** `(hand.html)`
> Four empty marks sit on the page. Empty — but not nameless.

**33. care** `(care.html)`
> A chess knight knows the only way across these squares.

**34. the cursor** `(cursor.html)`
> Move across the panel. A second arrow runs a half-step ahead of yours.

**35. foresight** `(foresight.html)`
> You can't read this from below. You have to go up first.

**36. the surface** `(surface.html)`
> Almost out. A cipher waits — and its key is hidden in the page itself.

**37. daybreak** `(qnloernx.html)`
> The first breath of each line, top to bottom.

**38. persist** `(persist.html)`
> There's a record here the screen keeps hidden.

**39. the witness** `(witness.html)`
> The smallest word, read through the glass.

**40. hope (the ending)** `(slkv.html)`

**41. deeper** `(deeper.html)`
