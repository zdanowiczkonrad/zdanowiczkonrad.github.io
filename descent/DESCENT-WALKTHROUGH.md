## Meta layers

- **404 / the wrong door** (`/404.html`): mistype a `/descent/` room and the page reacts — ‘that door is real, you mistyped’ / ‘close, one or two letters off’ / ‘cold’, and it counts your wrong guesses.
- **Map-reduce → the 41st room**: every room's `<pre id="art">` carries one `accesskey` letter. In chain order they spell `WALK PAST FORTY · THE FOREST KEEPS ONE DOOR DEEPER` → **deeper.html**.
- **Return to the surface** (room 35): visiting the homepage sets `localStorage[descent.surfaced]`, which unlocks the word on `foresight.html`.
- **Anniversary**: on **June 24** (the lore date) `deeper.html` burns through one extra line.
