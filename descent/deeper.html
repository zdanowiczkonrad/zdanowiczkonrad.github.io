Forty rooms was the first lid.

You gathered a letter from each — one trick per room,
a key pressed in the source — and they spelled the way here:

  walk past forty · the forest keeps one door deeper

This is that door. There is no exit riddle now.
The ember is yours; carry it as deep as you like.
The light does not win. It persists. So do you.
forty rooms each kept a single letter; you have been spelling this since the first door.
ten doors on the way down wore small marks. each mark keeps the first sound of its door's name.
and the voices beneath the rooms spoke in order too, if you kept their first words.
