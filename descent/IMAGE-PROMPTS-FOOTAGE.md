For the **leet pages** specifically (tresed, dneps, sthgiew, kindling): show the **plaintext clue word** with its encodable letters faded, plus a substitution key — the row `o i e a` over `0 1 3 4` (o→0, i→1, e→3, a→4) — and never the assembled number-word. For shift/mirror ciphers (reverse, rot13, atbash, caesar) show a dial or reflection, never the word. For hex/braille/morse/tally show a small reference legend, never the answer. Example/legend content must not spell any other descent word.
